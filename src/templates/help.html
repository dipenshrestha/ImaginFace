How to use ImaginFace?
Step 1 : Write a Prompt

Use your imagination to craft an original line of text. Provide as much detail as possible for the best result. We use CelebA dataset that comprises images of celebrities, each annotated with 43 binary attributes, such as "Bald," "Bangs," "Eyeglasses," "Smiling," and "Sad," among others. These attributes describe facial characteristics and features present in the images.

Step 2 : Generate Image

Once you have written the description, you are good to go. Just click the Generate button, and the image will be generated.
